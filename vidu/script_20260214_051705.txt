There is a lie that the world whispers to you when you turn 60, 70, or 80.
Can you hear it?
It whispers: "Your time is up. You are fading.
Step back and let the young take over."
We live in a culture that is obsessed with beginnings.
It worships the sunrise but fears the sunset. It worships speed, youth, and productivity.
And because of this, the world tells you a cruel story:
It says that if you cannot run fast, you have no value. If you are not producing, you are perishing.
But I want you to listen to me very carefully today, because I am going to tell you the truth that society is too blind to see. God operates on a different clock.
While the world looks at your aging body and sees decline, God looks at your enduring spirit and sees a weapon being sharpened. You are not becoming weaker, my friend; you are becoming concentrated.
